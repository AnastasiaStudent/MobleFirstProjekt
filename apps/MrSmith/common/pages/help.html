Start
On the Start site enter your master-password. If you have forgotten your password you can reset MrSmith with one click on red cross. WARNING: all data will be deleted!!!
Initialization
On the Initialization site enter your master-password twice and remember it.
All logins
From this site you can navigate to your preferred web-site. Here you see all your recorded services. To select any of them you should click on its icon.
User
On this site are all your listed accounts from your selected service before. Now you can navigate to your preferred web-site.
Browser
With just one click you are logged in and can use your service. With just one click on the button close you can leave your service. And you can use MrSmith again.
Manage list
On this site you can edit all service properties. To do this you should click on one of the services. Also You can add a new login. To do this click on the green cross in the right corner of the screen.
Manage User
On this site are all your listed accounts from your selected service before. Now you can edit one of the accounts. To select any of them you should click on its icon.
Edit account
On this site you can show your current password or delete this account permanently.
New account
After clicking the PLUS-Icon you can add an account by using a specific service from the default-list.
Options
If you want initialize this App you can restore the factory-defaults. WARNING: ALL DATA WILL BE LOST! Make sure that you changed all passwords on external websites by your own before restoring this APP!
